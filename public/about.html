About JC Currency Converter

Our Mission

JC Currency Converter aims to provide a simple, reliable, and user-friendly currency conversion tool accessible globally without requiring login or fees.

Features

  • Supports multiple global currencies with up-to-date exchange rates
  • Simple and intuitive interface with large numeric keypad
  • No registration or login required for conversions
  • Responsive design optimized for both desktop and mobile devices

Privacy & Data

We do not collect or store any personal information. Exchange rate data is fetched from trusted third-party APIs to ensure accuracy.
